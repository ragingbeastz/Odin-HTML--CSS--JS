Pasta Bolognese

Description

Pasta Bolognese is a classic Italian dish made with a rich and hearty meat sauce that is simmered for hours to develop deep flavors. It is traditionally served with tagliatelle pasta, but you can use any pasta shape you like. This recipe is easy to make and perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 500g pasta of your choice
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, to serve
  • Fresh basil, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour in red wine and simmer until reduced by half.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, beef stock, and bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  8. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  9. Remove bay leaf from sauce and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  10. Drain pasta and toss with sauce.
  11. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
