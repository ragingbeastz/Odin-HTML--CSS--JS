Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch of Ragu
  • 1 batch of Cheese Sauce
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Use a 33 x 22 x 7 cm / 13 x 9 x 2.5″ baking dish.
  2. Smear a bit of Ragu on the base, then cover with lasagna sheets. Tear sheets to fit.
  3. Spread over 2 1/2 cups of Ragu (enough to cover sheets), then drizzle over 1 cup of Cheese Sauce.
  4. Top with lasagna sheets (Note 7). Spread with another 2 1/2 cups of Ragu, then 1 cup of Cheese Sauce. Top with lasagna sheets then repeat 1 more time.
  5. Top with a 4th layer of lasagna sheets, then pour over the remaining Cheese Sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with Mozzarella, then bake for 25 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
  7. Stand for 5 to 10 minutes before cutting and serving, garnished with basil or parsley if desired.
  8. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F.
